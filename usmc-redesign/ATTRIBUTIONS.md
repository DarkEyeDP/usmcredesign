## Data Sources

All official data used in this tool is sourced from U.S. Government publications
and is in the public domain.

- **MARADMIN messages** — U.S. Marine Corps ([marines.mil](https://www.marines.mil))
- **Basic pay tables** — Defense Finance and Accounting Service ([dfas.mil](https://www.dfas.mil))
- **FY27 SRBP bonus data** — MARADMIN 030/25, marines.mil
- **MOS descriptions** — NAVMC 1200.1L, U.S. Marine Corps
